The Browser History provides a list of constants and assertions that have been recently accessed by the current user in the current CYC® image.

Clicking on the "Clear History" link will remove all constants and assertions that are currently in the KB Browser History, returning the Browser History to its initial state.

The KB Browser History is divided into two sections:

  • "Recent Constants": lists the CYC® constants most recently viewed in this CYC® image.

  • "Recent Assertions": lists the CYC® assertions most recently viewed in this CYC® image.
